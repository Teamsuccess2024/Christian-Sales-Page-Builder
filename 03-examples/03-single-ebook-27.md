**CRITICAL LOW-TICKET PRINCIPLE (Read Before the Page)**

A \$27 sales page is not a shrunken \$97 sales page. It's a fundamentally different animal.

At \$27, the buyer's decision is largely emotional and impulse-adjacent. The page needs to **move fast, hit hard, and not over-sell.** Long-form sales pages at this price point actually _lower_ conversion because they make the reader think too hard about a decision that should be quick.

Four rules for low-ticket Christian sales pages:

- **Shorter is stronger.** Aim for one-third the length of a \$97 page.
- **Lead with the transformation, not the testimony.** No time for a long origin story.
- **One core method does the heavy lifting** - usually Method 2 (Big Idea) or Method 4 (Transformation Bridge). Other methods support but don't compete.
- **Stewardship framing is critical.** \$27 should feel like an obvious yes, not a careful financial decision.

Total deployed methods on a \$27 page: typically 5-6 of the 10, not all of them.

**SECTION 4 - The Hook**

_\[METHOD 1: Testimony-Driven PAS, but compressed - a \$27 page can't afford a long opener\]_

Joseph had a dream at seventeen.

He didn't sit on the throne until he was thirty.

**That's thirteen years.** Thirteen years between the promise and the position. Thirteen years of betrayal by his brothers, slavery in Potiphar's house, false accusation by Potiphar's wife, prison for a crime he didn't commit, and - the cruelest cut - being _forgotten_ by the cupbearer he helped, for two more full years after that.

Most Christians know the end of Joseph's story. The throne. The grain. The brothers brought to their knees. _What you intended for evil, God intended for good._

**But almost no one teaches the middle.**

The middle is where you live.

The middle is where the dream God gave you collides with the reality you're walking through. The middle is where you start asking whether you heard Him wrong in the first place. The middle is where most believers either become bitter, or become Joseph.

This book is about the middle.

_"You intended to harm me, but God intended it for good to accomplish what is now being done, the saving of many lives."_ - Genesis 50:20 (NIV)

The Joseph pattern is this: **the dream is real, the prison is the path, and the position is on the other side of years you didn't sign up for.**

- **Method 4 - Transformation Bridge:** Section 7
- **Method 6 - Value Stack with Stewardship:** Sections 6, 9 (compressed)

- **Method 9 - Objection-Crushing FAQ:** Section 13 (5 questions max - not 9)
- **Method 10 - Risk Reversal + Kingdom Urgency:** Sections 10, 12, 14

**Methods deliberately omitted from a \$27 page:**

- **Method 5 - Curated Library Frame:** Not relevant for a single book offer. Light mention only in the FAQ.

**Key Low-Ticket Principles Demonstrated**

For Claude's pattern recognition, here are the specific low-ticket adaptations this page makes:

1. **One Big Idea carries the page** (Section 2, Section 5) - at \$27, you don't need to layer multiple frames. Pick the sharpest one.
2. **The opener is compressed** (Section 4) - no long origin story. Get to the hook fast.
3. **One belief broken, not three** (Section 8) - multiple objection-breaks slow the buy at low price points.
4. **Stewardship framing makes the price feel obvious** (Section 9) - "less than a restaurant meal" is the anchor.
5. **Short FAQ** (Section 13) - only the questions that actually slow the sale. No "what denomination is this" - that's for higher-ticket consideration buyers.
6. **Single P.S.** (Section 15) - no stacked P.S./P.P.S. Direct, brief.
7. **Total page length** is roughly **40% of the \$97 bundle page** - this is the right ratio for the price difference.

**When to Use a \$27 Single-Book Offer in a Funnel**

Single-book \$27 offers typically serve one of three roles:

1. **Front-end / Lead-Generating Tripwire** - A low-friction first purchase that converts cold traffic into buyers, who can then be ascended to the \$97 bundle and \$297 library.
2. **Standalone Topical Entry** - For visitors arriving via specific topical search ("Joseph Genesis study," "Christian waiting season") who aren't yet ready for a bundle commitment.
3. **Gift / Share-Friendly Product** - A price point low enough that buyers will purchase one for a friend in a specific season.

The \$27 single-book page should always have a **post-purchase upsell** to the \$97 bundle or \$297 library. The methodology for that upsell follows the same frame-shift principles demonstrated in library-upsell-297.md, adjusted for the smaller jump.
