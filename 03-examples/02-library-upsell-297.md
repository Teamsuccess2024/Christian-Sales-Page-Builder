**CRITICAL UPSELL PRINCIPLE (Read Before the Page)**

The single biggest mistake on Christian upsell pages is treating them like a second sales page. They're not. The buyer has already made a buying decision. The friction is gone. The card is in. The trust is built.

The upsell's job is **frame-shift**, not re-sell. You are not convincing them to buy. You are showing them something they didn't know was available - and making it feel obvious to say yes.

Three rules for every upsell page:

- **Acknowledge the purchase first.** Never make the buyer feel like the sale isn't done.
- **Shift the frame, don't repeat the pitch.** New problem, new solution, new identity.
- **Make the math obvious.** The price-per-unit on the upsell should feel ridiculous compared to the primary offer.
